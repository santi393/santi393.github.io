What We Do

Three Asset Classes.
One Regenerative Thesis.

Grounded in institutional-grade advisory expertise, SALVA deploys capital across three interconnected verticals where risk-adjusted returns and measurable regenerative impact converge: Regenerative Tourism, Mixed Use Residential, and Resilient Infrastructure. Our portfolio companies operate across the Dominican Republic, Costa Rica, Mexico, and the United States.

01
Regenerative
Tourism

Destination hospitality assets developed in partnership with world-class operators and locally-rooted management teams. Every project generates measurable environmental and community benefit alongside competitive financial returns.

02
Mixed Use
Residential

Institutional-grade branded residential communities with positive long-term social, environmental, and economic impacts. Structured to appreciate capital through cap rate compression and generate systematic, cumulative returns.

03
Resilient
Infrastructure

Technology licensing and infrastructure financing that introduces proven sustainable solutions into emerging markets. Clean energy, water systems, and impact technology that enhances destination resilience and reduces long-term operating cost.

The SALVA Regeneration Fund

Six Pillars.
Where Principles Become Practice.

These six regeneration pillars underpin every design, development, and investment decision across the SALVA portfolio. They interface directly with the independent SALVA Regeneration Fund, a donor-advised fund administered through CAF Americas, which supports qualified third-party non-profit organizations whose work actively restores, regenerates, and enhances these pillars within the communities surrounding our projects.

View Our Fund at CAF Americas
Resource Circularity
Closed-loop resource systems embedded across every phase of design, development, and active operations, minimizing waste and maximizing regenerative potential at each site.
Accelerating Climate Solutions
Carbon-neutral design, renewable energy, and climate adaptation strategies integrated from pre-acquisition through exit, future-proofing every asset in our portfolio.
Developing Community Prosperity
Local employment, supplier networks, and social impact programs cultivated through every cycle of investment, development, and operation.
Natural Systems Stewardship: Land & Sea
Land and marine habitat conservation woven into site selection, development management, active operations, and long-term asset stewardship.
Enhancing Health & Well-being
Guest and community well-being programs activated at every stage, from pre-opening programming through ongoing outreach, creating lasting human impact.
Preserving Culture, Supporting the Arts
Heritage preservation, arts programming, and cultural catalysts embedded in the social fabric of each destination from day one through operations.
